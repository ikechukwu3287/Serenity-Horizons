Welcome to Serenity Horizons

Serenity Horizons is a premium Community-Based Residential Facility (CBRF) located in Wauwatosa, Milwaukee County, offering assisted living and specialized memory care services for middle-to-upper-income seniors.

With a capacity for 16 residents, Serenity Horizons combines luxurious, home-like accommodations with personalized care plans, ensuring the highest quality of life for its residents. Our facility is strategically positioned near the Milwaukee Regional Medical Center, providing easy access to healthcare services and catering to the growing senior population in the area.

Our Mission

To provide compassionate, premium care in a safe and nurturing environment, empowering seniors to live with dignity and independence.
